What does Monitored Mean?

The purpose of this block is to encourage students to manage their time effectively. Each student can monitor their progress in completing the activies and resources you have created.

On the configuration page you will see a list of all the modules that you have created which can be monitored by the Progress Bar block. The module will only be monitored and appear as a small block in the progress bar if a checkmark is placed next to the corresponding activity/resource.
