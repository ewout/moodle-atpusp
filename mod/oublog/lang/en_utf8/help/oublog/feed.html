Automatic notification

If you use a news reader program or website, you can add the Internet address of the Atom or RSS links in order to be updated when new posts are made to this blog.

If the blog allows comments to be made against individual posts, additional ‘Comments only’ Atom and RSS links will be available. You can use these to update you when a new comment has been added to any of the posts contained on this blog.

Your course doesn't require you to use this feature.

Atom and RSS

Atom and RSS are two formats for the same thing. The former is a technologically neater format; the latter is better known. Most news readers support both formats. Try the Atom link; if that doesn't work, try the RSS.
