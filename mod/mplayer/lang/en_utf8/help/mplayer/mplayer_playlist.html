Play List

Position:

  • none = no playlist
  • bottom = under the video window
  • right = to the right of the video window
  • over = playlist covers the screen, disappears when user plays video

Size: 0 - 9999 pixels

Start Item: 0 - 99

Repeat:

  • none = don't repeat
  • list = play entire playlist once
  • always = repeat entire playlist
  • single = repeat selected item on playlist

Shuffle: play playlist items in random order
