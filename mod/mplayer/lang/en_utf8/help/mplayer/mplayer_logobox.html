Logo Box

Align: alignment of logo

Image: JPG, GIF, PNG or SWF logo to display

Link: URL to open (new window) when user clicks on logo

Margin: area/border around logo

Position: when set to "over", the box background is hidden

For more information see: http://developer.longtailvideo.com/trac/wiki/PluginsLogobox
